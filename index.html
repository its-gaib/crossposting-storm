The Problem

How It Works

  • Compose your post in a single unified editor
  • Platform-aware content adaptation (length, hashtags, mentions)
  • Media transcoding & resizing per platform specs
  • Scheduled or instant publishing
  • Thread splitting for long-form content
  • Preview exactly what each platform will show
  • Analytics aggregated across all channels
  • API-first design — integrate with any workflow
